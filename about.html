Our Story

Born in
Addis.
Built for
the World.

SaFE Production was founded on a simple belief: that great content deserves great strategy. Too many brands were creating beautiful videos that no one saw — or chasing vanity metrics that didn't translate to real growth.

We set out to change that. By combining deep audience research with cinema-grade production, we created a new kind of social video agency — one where every frame is intentional, every post is optimised, and every campaign is designed to build lasting organic reach.

Today, we work with brands across East Africa and beyond, helping them turn social media from a cost centre into a growth engine. From strategy to distribution, we handle the entire journey — so our clients can focus on what they do best.
