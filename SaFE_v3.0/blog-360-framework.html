The 360 Content Framework Explained

SH
Samuel Hussien
Creative Lead
May 22, 2026 6 min read

Most content fails before a single frame is shot. Not because the idea is bad, or the execution poor — but because a critical step was skipped. At Premium+, we've developed a 360-degree framework that ensures every piece of content we produce has the best possible chance of success. Here's how it works.

Phase 1: Strategy

Before we touch a camera, we immerse ourselves in your brand, your audience, and your platform. Who are we speaking to? What do they care about? What content formats are they already engaging with? We build a data-backed content strategy that defines the what, why, and where of every piece of content.

"Strategy isn't a document — it's the foundation that every creative decision is built on."

Phase 2: Creative

With strategy in hand, our creative team develops concepts that align with your brand truth while speaking directly to your audience's interests. We don't chase trends — we find the intersection between what's authentic to you and what resonates with them. Every concept is pitched with a clear distribution plan.

Phase 3: Content Capture

This is where the magic happens — but it's also where good preparation pays off. Our production team brings cinema-grade equipment and expertise to every shoot. We capture with the edit in mind, shooting for multiple formats and platforms simultaneously to maximise efficiency.

Phase 4: Post Production

Raw footage is just the beginning. Our editors, colourists, sound designers, and motion graphics artists transform captured content into polished deliverables. Each version is optimised for its target platform — different pacing for TikTok vs YouTube, different aspect ratios, different caption styles.

Phase 5: Distribution

Great content means nothing if it doesn't reach the right people. We develop platform-specific distribution strategies — optimal posting times, hashtag strategies, cross-platform sequencing, and community engagement plans. Each platform gets a tailored approach.

Phase 6: Optimisation

The final phase is where most agencies stop — but it's where we start learning. We analyse every metric that matters: watch time, engagement rate, share velocity, conversion paths. These insights feed directly back into the next cycle of content, creating a continuous improvement loop.

Why It Works

The 360 framework works because it's holistic. Every phase informs every other phase. The strategy shapes the creative, the creative guides the production, the production enables the edit, the edit determines the distribution, and the distribution data drives the next strategy. It's a flywheel — and once it's spinning, it generates momentum that's hard to stop.

Ready to see the 360 framework in action? Let's talk about your next project.
