Cinema-Grade Video for Social: Our Setup

KK
Kirubel Kiendeye
Camera Operator
May 10, 2026 5 min read

Social media doesn't have to look like social media. At Premium+, we believe the content that performs best is the content that looks and feels like cinema — because audiences can feel the difference, even if they can't articulate it. Here's the gear, techniques, and workflows that let us deliver broadcast-quality content for every platform.

Camera & Lenses

We shoot primarily on the Sony FX6 and FX3 — cinema cameras that deliver stunning dynamic range, beautiful skin tones, and the kind of depth that makes viewers stop scrolling. Our lens kit ranges from wide-angle Sigma Art primes to telephoto zooms, letting us capture everything from intimate product details to sweeping establishing shots.

For social content, we often shoot in 4K even when delivering in 1080p — the extra resolution gives us flexibility in post for reframing, stabilising, and creating multiple compositions from a single take.

"The camera is just a tool. What matters is the eye behind it and the story in front of it."

Lighting

Lighting is where amateur work separates from professional work. We use a mix of Aputure and Godox LED panels, plus traditional fixtures for when we need that cinematic warmth. Every shoot gets a custom lighting plan — no two setups are the same because no two brands are the same.

For social shoots, we've developed a streamlined lighting kit that lets us set up in under 20 minutes without sacrificing quality. Speed matters when you're shooting multiple concepts in a day.

Movement

Static shots have their place, but movement brings energy. Our DJI RS 3 Pro gimbal (operated by Abenezer) gives us buttery-smooth tracking shots, while our slider and dolly setups provide controlled, deliberate motion. We choose movement based on the story — fast and dynamic for energy, slow and steady for emotion.

Audio

Bad audio kills good video. We use Rode Wireless PRO lav mics for interviews and the Zoom F3 field recorder for ambient capture. In post, every piece of audio gets cleaned, balanced, and enhanced. We also work with composers for original music when the project calls for it.

The Social Workflow

Here's what makes our setup unique: everything is designed for multi-platform output. Every shot is composed with reframing in mind — a wide landscape shot that becomes a tighter vertical crop for Reels. We capture in formats that let us deliver optimised versions for YouTube (landscape), Instagram (square), TikTok (vertical), and LinkedIn (landscape with captions) — all from a single production day.
