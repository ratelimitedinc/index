Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What is your typical project timeline?

Most projects range from 2 to 6 weeks depending on scope. A single campaign typically takes 3 weeks from strategy to final delivery. We'll give you a clear timeline during our first call.

Do you work with clients outside Ethiopia?

Absolutely. While we're based in Addis Ababa, we work with brands across East Africa and internationally. We can travel for shoots or coordinate with local crews in your market.

What does a full 360 package include?

Everything from initial strategy and creative development through production, post-production, distribution planning, and performance optimisation. It's our most comprehensive offering.

Can you help with just one part of the process?

Yes — we're happy to step in at any stage. Whether you need a distribution strategy for existing content or a production crew for your next shoot, we can support exactly what you need.
